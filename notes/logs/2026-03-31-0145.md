# Session Log: 2026-03-31 01:45 AM

## Topic
NIRSpec v8 Calibration Validation: Custom IFU Science Extractions and Full-Spectrum Reporting.

## Rationale
Standard pipeline `extract_1d` products for IFU science targets (UGC-5101, SDSSJ0841) use PSF-weighted extractions that may not capture the full source flux or may introduce systematic biases in extended wavelength regions. v8 implements a fixed 0.5" circular aperture centered on the peak pixel, consistent with Parlanti et al. (2025). Additionally, v8 enforces gap-aware plotting to prevent misleading interpolations over physical detector gaps.

## Findings
- **Extraction Ratios**: The 0.5" circular aperture returns ~75% (UGC-5101) and ~89% (SDSSJ0841) of the flux compared to the pipeline's default extraction at nominal wavelengths.
- **Wavelength Gaps**: Physical gaps at ~2.2 µm (G140M) and ~3.7 µm (G235M) are now correctly suppressed in full-spectrum plots.
- **Data Integrity**: All IFU and FS targets (1536, 1537, 1538, 2186, 2654, 6645, 1492, 6644) have verified v8 reductions and visualizations.

## Decisions Made
- Use **0.5" radius circular aperture** for science targets in IFU mode.
- Suppress plotting between wavelengths in the **detector gaps** (2.19–2.23 µm and 3.65–3.80 µm).
- Use `PIXAR_SR` from header for proper `MJy/sr` to `Jy` conversion in custom IFU extractions.
